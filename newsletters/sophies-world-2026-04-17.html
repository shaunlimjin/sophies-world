🤯 Weird But True

Nature Is Wonderfully Weird! 🌿

🦩 Flamingos Are Born GREY! Those gorgeous pink flamingos you've seen in photos? They actually hatch from their eggs completely grey and white. Their feathers slowly turn pink because of the shrimp and blue-green algae they eat — which are packed with special pink pigments called carotenoids. Stop eating shrimp, and a flamingo actually fades back to white! 🍤
🦦 Sloths Are Secret Breath-Holding Champions! Sloths look super slow hanging in trees — but underwater they are surprisingly incredible. A sloth can hold its breath for up to 40 minutes. That's four times longer than a dolphin, which can only manage about 10 minutes! Scientists think sloths slow their heart rate way down to save oxygen. Sneakily amazing! 🌊
🎲 Wombats Make Cube-Shaped Poop! Every animal on Earth makes round or oval droppings — except the Australian wombat. These marsupials produce perfectly cube-shaped poop! Scientists discovered their intestines are stretchy and irregularly shaped, which squishes waste into little cubes as it moves through. Wombats even stack the cubes to mark their territory — and cubes don't roll away! 🐨

🌍 World Watch

Big News From Around the Globe 🗺️

⛽ The World's Most Important Shipping Lane Is Open Again!

There's a narrow strip of ocean between Iran and the Arabian Peninsula called the Strait of Hormuz. About one in every five barrels of oil in the whole world travels through it on giant tanker ships. After weeks of conflict between the USA and Iran, ships were blocked — and oil prices shot up by more than 60%!

This week, the USA and Iran agreed to a two-week ceasefire — meaning both sides agreed to stop fighting. The strait is reopening, ships are moving again, and countries around the world breathed a huge sigh of relief. 😮‍💨

🍎 Think of it like this: Imagine the only road to your grocery store was suddenly blocked by a massive traffic jam. No delivery trucks could get through, so food prices shot way up. When the road finally cleared, trucks could roll again and prices could start coming back down. The Strait of Hormuz is like that road — but for oil!

📈 The World Economy Is Feeling the Squeeze

The International Monetary Fund (IMF) — a big organisation that watches over the world's money — released an important report this week. It says the global economy is slowing down because of the Middle East conflict. When oil gets expensive, everything connected to oil gets pricier too: petrol for cars, electricity, and even groceries (because farmers and delivery trucks need fuel).

The IMF now predicts the world economy will grow by only 3.1% in 2026 — slower than usual. Countries like Singapore and the USA are working together to keep prices from going too high for everyday families. 🤝

🧩 Think of it like this: Imagine your family's budget is like a jigsaw puzzle with lots of pieces — food, clothes, holidays, fun. When one important piece (like petrol) suddenly costs way more, the other pieces get much harder to fit in. That's exactly what's happening to families and governments all around the world right now.

🇸🇬 Singapore Spotlight

What's Happening in the Lion City! 🦁

🛢️ Singapore Is Watching Oil Prices Very Carefully

Singapore's Prime Minister Lawrence Wong spoke out this week about the Middle East conflict. He noted that oil prices have risen more than 60% since the war started — and Singapore gets a huge amount of its energy through ships passing near the Strait of Hormuz. As a small island with no oil of its own, Singapore depends entirely on imports. The ceasefire news is a really big deal! PM Wong is planning smart ways to keep energy steady and prices manageable for Singaporean families. 🏙️

💰 Money Moves

The 3-Jar Magic Trick 🫙🫙🫙

Here's one of the best money habits you can start right now — it's called the 3-Jar Method! Whenever you get pocket money, a birthday gift, or earn money from chores, divide it into three jars (or envelopes):

🫙 Jar 1 — Save (60%): This money grows over time. Save it for something big you really want, like new gymnastics gear or a special trip.

🫙 Jar 2 — Spend (30%): This is your fun money! Use it guilt-free on small treats or things you enjoy right now.

🫙 Jar 3 — Share (10%): Give this to someone who needs it — a charity, a school fundraiser, or a good cause. Giving actually feels amazing! 🌟

💡 Sophie's Tip: If you get $10, put $6 in Save, $3 in Spend, and $1 in Share. Do this every single time and your savings will grow surprisingly fast. Even saving just $6 a week adds up to over $300 in one year — just from your Save jar alone! 🚀

She created Luv Ur Skin, a natural skincare range designed just for children. At age 13, she appeared on Shark Tank and landed a $65,000 investment deal. She turned a simple observation into a real business using her savings and her passion. If Isabella can do it at 8, imagine what you could build! 💪
